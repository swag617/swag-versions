### 🤖 Discord Integration Upgrade! 🤖

We’ve completely rebuilt how our Minecraft server talks to Discord behind the scenes! This means faster syncing, cooler notifications, and a much smoother experience for everyone. Here is what you will notice:

*   **🔗 Seamless Account Linking:** We’ve upgraded our account linking database! Linking your Minecraft character to your Discord account is now faster and more reliable than ever. 
*   **💬 New `/discord` Command:** Need a quick invite link to our Discord server? Just type `/discord` in-game to get an instant invite!
*   **💀 Public Death Messages:** Watch your friends (or rivals) bite the dust in real-time! Player deaths are now beautifully broadcasted directly into Discord.
*   **🔨 Better Moderation Logs:** Punishments and staff actions are now logged more cleanly and instantly, keeping our community safe and transparent.
*   **🔔 Shiny New Embeds:** Discord notifications, alerts, and bot messages have received a major visual glow-up with clean, modern layouts and custom formatting.
*   **🛒 Auction House Alerts:** Buying and selling on the Auction House? We've improved how AH transactions are sent to Discord so you never miss a deal.
*   **⚡ Behind-the-Scenes Speed:** We integrated with our core server systems to ensure the Discord bot never causes in-game lag.

Hop on FleaMC today and link your account to try out the new features! 🚀
